How to get a driver's licence

Boqing Wang (2018-04-05)

You must have a driver's license to legally operate a motor vehicle in China. If you are new to driving, start with a learner's permit. Take your time and practice, and schedule your appointment for your driver's test when you're ready. Driver's licenses are issued by the Traffic Police Detachment Vehicle Management Office under the Public Security Bureau. You typically must pass a written, driving, and vision test before you can get your driver's license.
